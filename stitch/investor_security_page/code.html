The Architectural
Integrity of Capital.

Security at Woodfield Developments is not a feature; it is the foundation. We employ a multi-layered legal and financial structure designed to ensure your capital is secured against tangible assets with senior priority.

Core Philosophy

"We manage investor capital with the same institutional rigor as a Tier-1 lender, prioritizing return of capital over return on capital."

Security Workflow

payments
Step 01

Investor Contribution

Capital is transferred via secure wire to a dedicated Woodfield Trust Account.

account_balance
Step 02

Trust Account Hold

Funds are held by third-party legal counsel until all security documentation is registered.

description
Step 03

Mortgage on Title

A legal charge is registered against the physical property title in favor of the investor.

account_tree
Step 04

Disbursement

Funds are released solely for development milestones verified by independent cost consultants.

Senior Position

1st Mortgage Priority Status

Investors hold a senior secured position. In the financial hierarchy, your claim is satisfied before any developer equity or subordinate debt. This "First-In, First-Out" structure provides the highest level of protection available in private real estate lending.

The Default Protocol

While rare, we maintain a clear "Power of Sale" process to protect investor principal. If a default occurs, the legal charge allows for the immediate takeover of the property.

Action Timeline
Notice of Sale Issued Day 15
Redemption Period Ends Day 50
Liquidation / Sale of Asset Day 60+

Proceeds are distributed to 1st mortgage holders before all other parties.
